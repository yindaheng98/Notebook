题目In some countries, the criminal trials are shown on TV and the general public can watch them. Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?

Traditionally, criminal trials are not available to the general public, but in some countries, people can watch criminal trials on TV. This pattern has some advantages, but it also leads to some problems.

Most importantly, citizens have the right to know what has happened and was happening in their society. With the power of media, publicly accessible criminal trials can attract people's eyes and provide information
